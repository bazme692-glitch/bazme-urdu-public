## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **Favorites Data**: When you mark kalams (poetry) as favorites, this data is stored locally on your device using Hive database.
- **Search History**: Your search queries are temporarily cached to improve search performance.

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: We may collect information about your device including device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
- **Usage Data**: We collect information about how you interact with the app, such as features used, kalams viewed, and authors browsed.
- **Ad Interaction Data**: Through Google AdMob, we collect data related to ad impressions, clicks, and interactions for advertising purposes.

### 1.3 Firebase Services
We use Firebase Cloud Firestore to:
- Store and retrieve kalam (poetry) content
- Manage author information
- Organize categories and content structure

Firebase may collect:
- Analytics data about app usage
- Crash reports to improve app stability
- Performance monitoring data

- **App Functionality**: To provide you with access to Urdu poetry and literature content
